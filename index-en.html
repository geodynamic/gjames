Research Themes

– Dynamical systems applied to the environment:

detection of wet snow, avalanche debris and mountain lakes using radar and optical satellite imagery, image analysis using nonlinear iterative methods.

– Nonlinear problems in mechanics:

impacts and waves in granular media, nonlinear waves at frictional contact interfaces, internal waves in stratified fluids.

– Dynamical systems applied to biology:

localized oscillations in DNA, ionic waves along actin filaments.

– Fundamental study of nonlinear dynamical systems in finite and infinite dimensions:

differential equations on lattices, systems with advance-delay, partial differential equations, iterations of maps. Bifurcation phenomena, localized waves, excitable systems.
